Buldak noodle recipe

Description

This is a buldak noodle recipe I love in 2024. With lots of love and cheese.

It only takes 10 minutes to make, with high volume and nutrition. Arranged by me.

Ingredients

  • Buldak noodle
  • cheese of any kind
  • Seaweed
  • Egg yolk
  • Garlic powder
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Add the noodle in boiling water and cook for 5minutes.
  2. pour the excess water out and put it back into the pan.
  3. Add milk for extra creamyness and steam it for 15seconds.
  4. Put Cheese, Powder, Spicy sauce, Garlic powder and eggyolk.
  5. wrap them with sea weed or not and enjoy.
