Yaki onigiri recipe

Yaki onigiri is a japanese dish, made with riceball and your little pan.

With little additions yaki onigiri is crispy on the outside, and mochi like on the inside.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Cheese
  • Soy sauce
  • Black sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cook rice.
  2. Make a riceball and add cheese inside it.
  3. Put butter on the pan with soysauce and a little bit of sugar.
  4. Burn them a little bit.
  5. Put the rice ball in the pan and, shower it with the sauce.
  6. Wait until the riceball is crispy and fully coated with sauce on the outside.
