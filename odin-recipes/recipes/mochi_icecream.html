3 ingredient mochi icecream

Mochi icecream is a perfect snack for summer with a fluffy outside and creamy on the inside.

Perfect for late night snacks too since its only 3 ingredients and, easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Favorite ice cream
  • Glutinous rice flour
  • Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Scoop ice cream and put them in a freezer.
  2. Scoop another separated ice cream into a different bowl and, melt them.
  3. Mix the melted ice cream with flour.
  4. Microwave or steam the flour icecream and, oil ur hands to knead them until smooth and stretchy.
  5. Devide them and add corn starch to roll them out flat.
  6. Assemble the freezed icecream and mochi skin.
